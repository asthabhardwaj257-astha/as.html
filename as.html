About Me

I am an entrepreneur and practitioner working at the intersection of mental health, learning & development and safeguarding (POSH & POCSO). With years of hands-on experience delivering corporate wellbeing programs, school & community awareness drives, and leadership development workshops, my approach combines evidence-based psychology, advanced NLP techniques and practical policy interventions.

Philosophy & Mission

I believe in holistic development: equipping individuals and organizations with skills, psychological resilience and safe culture to thrive. My mission is to build accessible programs that create measurable impact in mental health awareness, workplace safety and learner empowerment.
